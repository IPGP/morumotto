Morumotto ?

モルモット (Mo-ru-mo-tto) is the japanese for Guinea Pig. Guinea Pigs natural diet is grass, but they can also feed on seeds providing that the seed respects a certain amount of requirements (not too small, not too hard...) to avoid choking hazard.[1] This software gathers seeds as well (as miniseed temporal traces, not actual seeds) and performs several quality control checks on it before storing them in a dedicated archive. This name is also close to the spanish words terremoto and maremoto which are the main purpose of having clean and continuous miniseed data, in order to study those phenomenons.
And, last but not least, it uses several frameworks named after natural things, like RabbitMQ, Flower and so on, so we thought about giving it a name to live in this environment, trying to avoid eating wild Celery which could be deadly for it [1] (or create bugs in our task manager), or being swallowed by a mean Python.

[1] Few informations about guinea pigs were gathered here : smallpetselect.com/can-guinea-pigs-eat-seeds
